Breeding System

  • Same-gen breeding within species creates next generation
  • Stats inherited via formula from parents
  • Moves can be inherited if child can learn them
  • Long cooldown between breeding sessions
  • Gen 4 created by cross-species Gen 3 breeding (e.g., Quetzalcoatl = Avian + Reptile)

Stats & Growth

Stat Affects Grows By
STR HP, Power, Max Energy Attacking
INT Special Power, Command Obedience, Move Learning Using abilities
AGI Movement Speed, Reaction Time Moving around
END HP, Energy Replenishment Rate Taking hits

Birthmarks

Random at birth. Affect personality, Core AI archetype, and stat growth modifiers (ranging from -20% to +20%).

Growth Decay

No levels - stats grow from battle actions. Growth rate decays as stats increase. Lower gens hit ceiling faster.

Combat System

Battle Format

  • 100% Real-time combat
  • 1v1, 1v2, and 2v2 modes
  • Player controls 2 monsters
  • Top-down / 2.5D camera

Monster Autonomy

  • Hidden obedience stat = f(INT, Temperament)
  • Low INT = can't understand orders
  • Low Temperament = won't obey
  • Ignored commands follow Core AI

Move System

  • 8 slots: 4 basic + 4 special
  • Physical, Magic, Buff, Debuff, Heal, AoE
  • Energy regenerates (slows over time)
  • Learn from: parents, stat thresholds, watching

Status Effects

  • Poison / DoT
  • Stun / Freeze
  • Slow
  • Blind / Accuracy debuff
  • Burn

Capture & Taming

Tranquilize

  • Monster must be below 25% HP
  • 50% success at 25% HP
  • 100% success at 5% HP or less
  • Can wake up if battle continues

Carry Back

  • Movement speed halved
  • Can't use items in battle
  • Death = lose the monster
  • Wild monsters start with low temperament

World & Arena

Island World

  • 4 themed regions (one per species)
  • Start in Insect region
  • ~20 screens wilderness per region
  • Caves and structures with bosses
  • Legendary Gen 4 + Gen 3 secret battles

Arena System

  • Ranks D through S per arena
  • Bracket tournaments + ladder
  • Rank B unlocks next area
  • Rank S can skip areas
  • No weight classes

Rewards

  • Gold currency
  • Items and ingredients
  • Food for growth buffs
  • Area access
  • Possibly monsters from arena

Base

  • Start with 6 monster capacity
  • Upgradable to 20 max
  • NPCs can move in (shops)
  • Enhances monster happiness/growth
